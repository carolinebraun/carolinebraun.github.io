Interests

I love to travel. I'll sit through a week-long conference if it means I get to see a new city. Adventure is awesome. In summer 2017, I loved backpacking Europe as well as working on a microfinance project with a local textile and ecotourism business in Cusco, Peru.

I love my friends. They're the best people I know. Who else would visit Niagara Falls in December? Celebrate New Year's in a parking lot? Get me to run a mini marathon?

I love filmmaking. I'm never going to be the creative genius, but I love helping with the logistics that make someone's vision come to life. Production is dynamic and all-consuming like nothing else.

I love rabbits. As a child, my family had every kind of small animal, from hedgehogs to chinchillas. I petsit whenever I get the opportunity. Even if it's an injured bat we found in a parking lot.

I love cooking. Especially pasta. If it has lemon, pesto or garlic, it's going to be good, and cooking is an amazing stress reliever.
